Technologies

Hello, welcome to my personal portfolio! I am a recent graduate from the coding bootcamp, App Academy, and here are some of the technologies that I have experience working with!

React

I have used React multiple times throughout my career as a web developer. I was introduced to the technology during my internship at Saight Dev where I worked on building websites for small businesses in the greater Austin area. I also used React for multiple projects during my time at App Academy.

Redux

I have used Redux to manage state with React for all of my projects with App Academy. I have developed proficiency with manipulating the Redux store to render desired information on the client-side.

Docker

Docker containerization has allowed me to easily deploy two of my projects. The concept of containerization fascinates me and I am excited to continue learning more about how to use Docker as effectively as possible. I am also learning how to use Kubernetes in my free time.

Flask

I used a flask backend for my final two projects and I quickly realized how much I enjoyed using it. I quickly developed proficiency by designing complicated data models and tables for both applications, Planstagram and Red Riding Hood. I used the ORM SQLAlchemy to query the databases, and working with this backend framework made me discover my passion for writing Python!
